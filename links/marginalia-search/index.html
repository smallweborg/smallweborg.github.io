Marginalia Search

https://search.marginalia.nu/

A less noisy, text-focused search engine.

It is a search engine, designed to help you find what you didn’t even know you were looking for. If you search for “Plato”, you might for example end up at the Canterbury Tales. Go looking for the Canterbury Tales, and you may stumble upon Neil Gaiman’s blog. (About)
